Decision Tree

Q1. Is it green, black, or pink?
YES → ISOLATE. Move tub away from clean tubs immediately.
Q2. Larger than a quarter or spreading fast?
YES → TERMINATE. Bag, seal, dispose outdoors.
NO → SPOT TREAT. H2O2 cotton, monitor 24 h.
Q3. Pin set already triggered?
YES → RUSH HARVEST. Pull mature pins early to bank yield.
NO → WATCH. Re-check every 12 h, photo log.
Q4. White fluff or blue bruising only?
→ NORMAL. Continue grow. Note in observations.
